Majuli

Explore the vibrant culture and beauty of Majuli!

About Majuli

Majuli is the largest river island in the world, located in the Brahmaputra River in Assam, India. It's known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse ecosystems, and scenic beauty.

Attractions

Some major attractions in Majuli include:

  • Vaishnavite Sattras
  • Traditional Assamese Dance Performances
  • Majuli's Unique Festivals
  • Birdwatching at the Island's Wetlands
  • Stunning River Views

Cuisine

Majuli's cuisine is reflective of Assamese culture, featuring a variety of unique dishes. Some popular items include:

  • Fish Tenga (Sour Fish Curry)
  • Masor Tenga (Tangy Fish Dish)
  • Thukpa (Noodle Soup)
  • Pitha (Rice Cakes)
  • Assamese Duck Curry
