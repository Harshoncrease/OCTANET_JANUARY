About Rajasthan

Rajasthan is the largest state in India, known for its desert landscapes, majestic forts, and vibrant culture.

Attractions

Key attractions in Rajasthan include:

  • Jaipur (Pink City)
  • Udaipur (City of Lakes)
  • Jaisalmer (Golden City)
  • Mehrangarh Fort
  • Hawa Mahal

Cuisine

Rajasthani cuisine is rich and varied, with a focus on vegetarian dishes. Notable dishes include:

  • Dal Baati Churma
  • Gatte ki Sabzi
  • Ker Sangri
  • Laal Maas
  • Rajasthani Kachoris
