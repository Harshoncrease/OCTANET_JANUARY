About Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh is a northern state in India, known for its rich history, diverse culture, and significant religious sites.

Attractions

Major attractions in Uttar Pradesh include:

  • The Taj Mahal
  • Varanasi Ghats
  • Agra Fort
  • Fatehpur Sikri
  • Ayodhya

Cuisine

Uttar Pradesh cuisine is known for its delicious flavors and rich dishes. Some popular dishes include:

  • Tandoori Roti
  • Chaat
  • Awadhi Biryani
  • Paneer Tikka
  • Jalebi
