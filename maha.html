About Maharashtra

Maharashtra is a state in the western region of India. It is the third largest state by area and the second largest state by population. The state capital is Mumbai, which is the financial capital of India.

Attractions

Some of the major attractions in Maharashtra include:

  • Gateway of India
  • Ajanta and Ellora Caves
  • Shirdi Sai Baba Temple
  • Mahabaleshwar Hill Station
  • Kolhapur and its heritage

Cuisine

Maharashtrian cuisine is known for its variety and flavors. Some popular dishes include:

  • Puran Poli
  • Vada Pav
  • Pav Bhaji
  • Misal Pav
  • Modak
