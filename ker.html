About Kerala

Kerala is located in the southern part of India, known for its lush greenery, backwaters, and unique culture.

Attractions

Top attractions in Kerala include:

  • Alleppey Backwaters
  • Munnar Hill Station
  • Fort Kochi
  • Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Thekkady (Periyar National Park)

Cuisine

Kerala's cuisine is famous for its seafood and coconut-based dishes. Popular dishes include:

  • Appam and Stew
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry
  • Fish Curry
  • Karimeen Pollichathu
  • Payasam
